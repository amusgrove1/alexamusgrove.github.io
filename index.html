Welcome to Prairie Fields!

Prairie Fields HOA is an organization of and for the owners of property in Prairie Fields subdivision, Savoy. Prairie Fields is regulated by covenants covering each phase of the development and currently operates under the HOA Bylaws. The day to day operations are run by an all-volunteer Board of Directors along with help from many committee volunteers. The Board establishes the annual dues assessment and is responsible for using the money assessed to maintain and improve HOA property and offer programs which are of value to Prairie Fields residents, thereby increasing the value of the neighborhood.
